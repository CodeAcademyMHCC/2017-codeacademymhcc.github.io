When working on a webdsite you'll need a way to connect to the server and upload or download your files. Some IDEs will have this tool built-in, but standalone tools can also be used.

Filezilla
An open-source (S)FTP client that's been around a long time.
Cyberduck
This client lets you connect to a variety of resources beyond (S)FTP, including Amazon S3, Google Drive, Dropbox and more.
Transmit ( )
A client that lets you connect to (S)FTP and other services as well. Made locally in Portland by Panic.
